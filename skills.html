Meditation

I can teach you about meditation and the benefits it has. Meditating can help you relax by calming the mind. It helps you manage your body's response to stressors by making you feel a sense of tranquility. Additionally regular meditation enhances attention span and concentration, which ultimately will help you stay focused on tasks and activities in your daily life.

In order to start basic meditation it is pretty simple. First be seated with your legs crossed and fingers placed near your thighs. Close your eyes and start with focusing your breath. Take slow and deep breaths. If you prefer, you can listen to a guided meditation to help you feel more relaxed. Continue doing this for about 5 minutes.
